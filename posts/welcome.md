# Welcome: How this portal works

This portal is a simple, fast website for visitors in **San Cristóbal de las Casas**.

## Events
- Open **Events** to filter by date/category/price.
- Click **Details** to see full info.

## Submit an event
Use the submission button on the home page.  
(You can connect it to a Google Form.)
